Crops


A crop is a plant that can be grown and harvested extensively for profit or subsistence.
When the plants of the same kind are cultivated at one place on a large scale, it is called a crop.
Most crops are cultivated in agriculture or aquaculture. A crop may include macroscopic fungus, or macroscopic marine alga.
